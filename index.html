Who We Are

About InAmigos Foundation

A Section 8 non-profit organization dedicated to bridging gaps in education, healthcare, and economic opportunities

🎯

Our Mission

To empower underprivileged communities through collective action, creating sustainable solutions that address education, healthcare, and economic development needs across India.

🌟

Our Vision

A world where every individual has access to quality education, healthcare, and opportunities to build a dignified life, regardless of their socioeconomic background.

💎

Our Values

Transparency, compassion, integrity, and community-driven approach guide everything we do. We believe in empowering communities rather than creating dependencies.

📍 Based in Chhattisgarh, India
📜 NITI Aayog Registered
✅ ISO 9001:2015 Certified
🏛️ 80G, 12A & CSR-1 Certified

Our Impact

Social Impact & Purpose

Creating measurable change in communities across India

📚

Education Access

Providing quality education to 500+ children through our Bachpanshala project, breaking the cycle of illiteracy in rural areas.

🏥

Healthcare Support

Organizing regular health camps and providing medical assistance to communities with limited access to healthcare facilities.

👩‍💼

Women Empowerment

Training 200+ women in vocational skills, enabling financial independence and creating sustainable livelihoods.

🌱

Environmental Action

Planted over 10,000 trees and organized numerous cleanliness drives, contributing to a greener and cleaner environment.
